How to Play Monster Survivors

The Objective

Monster Survivors is an intense action-survival game where your primary goal is to stay alive as long as possible while battling endless waves of increasingly dangerous monsters. Each wave brings new challenges, stronger enemies, and the opportunity to become more powerful through strategic upgrades.

Core Mechanics

Navigate through dynamic battlefields using intuitive controls while automatically attacking nearby enemies. Your character grows stronger by collecting experience orbs dropped by defeated monsters. Every level gained allows you to choose from powerful upgrades that can dramatically change your playstyle.

  • • Movement: Use WASD or arrow keys to dodge enemy attacks
  • • Combat: Your character auto-attacks the nearest enemy
  • • Upgrades: Choose wisely from randomized power-ups each level

Enemy Types

Face diverse monster types, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns. From swarming minions that overwhelm with numbers to powerful bosses that require strategic positioning, every enemy presents a different challenge. Learning their patterns is key to survival.
